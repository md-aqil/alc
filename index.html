Filtering
Indicate how many new connections you’d like each day. If there are additional incoming conversations, users attach stars to prioritize their first message
Sorting
Name will use machine learning to find you the best matches. Algorithms are trained via your behaviour in platform, and refined with opt-in feedback from your experience in person.
Connecting
Incoming connections with the most Stars get through, and the Stars are transferred directly to your wallet or to a charity of your choosing. No Stars are needed after the first message.
Calibrating
When meeting in person, you’re invited to verify the meeting and provide compatibility feedback. This data is anonymously processed internally to train our machine learning algorithms on what matters — quality of connection. As Name grows, so does the fidelity of each connection.
